Google Chrome in Material Design

The Google Chrome Browser hasn't seen a redesign since inception. I think its time for Chrome to be redesigned. Here's what I think would look like a good redesign with all the Material Love.

The active tabs make the title color white, gets a darker shade of the theme color and a white underline. The inactive ones don't get the underline and the text is grey.

The + fab button moves with the number of tabs and sticks next to the username when the tab-bar is full.

The username is itself the button. Click on the username for changing profile.

The search bar gets blue colored when active. When inactive, its grey.

Reduced the 8 "Most visited site" tiles to 5.


Youtube in Material Design

With every Google entity turing to Material Design, Youtube hasn't got the love yet.

Hence I made what I think would look like a Material Design Youtube for Web.

The video cards have got a bottom color. This color would depend on the content thumbnail and will change its color using something like the Palette API for Android.
